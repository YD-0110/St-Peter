St Peter the Apostle

Who is St Peter?

St Peter as one of the twelve apostles of Jesus. He was the first bishop and pope of Rome. Jesus gave him "the keys of the kingdom of heaven," which is why he is frequently seen at the heavenly gates in popular art and culture.



Names that represent St Peter:

- Peter the Apostle
- Peter the Rock
- Simon Peter
- Simeon
- Simon
- Cephas



Appearances of St Peter in the stations of the light:

Station 2: Peter and another Disciple run towards the tomb of Jesus and see that the tomb is empty.
Station 9: Jesus told Peter to bring fish that he caught. Peter brought all 153 large fish in the net. Jesus then said "Come, lets have breakfast". The Disciples didn't ask any questions because they knew that it was Jesus.



Gifts of St Peter:

- Understanding: Peter Understood the situation that Jesus was in when he was crucified. He knew that Jesus died for us.
- Knowledge: Peter had the the knowwledge to perform the first miracle after pentecost.
- Fear of the Lord: Peter highly respected the Lord wwith glory, majesty amnd purity.
- Wisdom: Peter calmly recounted all that occurred to him from start to finish, allowing the others to comprehend what had happened for themselves and converting them to God's plan of redemption for everyone who would believe.



Fruits of St Peter:

- Love: Peter was loved Jesus the most out of any disciple.
- Peace: Peter was very peaceful which resulted in having a better relationship with God.
- Faithfulness: Peter had faith that Jesus would send the Holy Spirit to share the good news.
- Self Control: Peter as able to lead the apostles and lead the catholic church as the first pope.
